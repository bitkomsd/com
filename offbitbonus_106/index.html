Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I register multiple devices and mine cryptocurrency on Bitcoin Bonus?

    On our service, you can register no more than 5 devices from one account. You can track earnings statistics for each device in your personal cabinet.

  • Can't log in to my personal account, what to do?

    If your device was previously registered on Bitcoin Bonus, login to your personal account will be available even if you don't remember your login credentials. This was made possible thanks to modern IP memorization systems.

  • My phone mined cryptocurrency without my actions related to your service. Why does it show that my phone has been mining all this time, can I get that money?

    At the moment, such cases have become more frequent. This may be related to fraudulent actions and hacking of mobile phones using virus programs. In this way, attackers extract information about your phone and register it on Bitcoin Bonus. Facing this problem in 2021, we developed an IP memorization and re-registration system. That is, if your phone was used by attackers to mine cryptocurrency without your consent, you can, by logging into the service from this phone, automatically rewrite the device's IP to yours and withdraw the earned funds. And the attackers will forever lose all data from your device and will no longer be able to log into your personal account.

  • How do I exchange mined bitcoins for my currency and withdraw the earned funds?

    Our service cooperates with the official cryptocurrency exchange service Binance. The exchange rate of Bitcoin and other currencies can be viewed in your personal account. For your convenience, we have made it possible to withdraw funds directly on our website. Just contact a manager and they will help you withdraw funds within 10 minutes.

  • What does my income depend on?

    Your income will depend on the power of your phone or tablet. Also, your income will be affected by the Bitcoin exchange rate at the time of sale. In order not to lose a lot of money, you can always write to a manager or use the online chat in your personal account.

  • My phone drains quickly, can I disable auto-collection of bitcoins?

    Our service uses no more than 30% of your smartphone's power for computational operations to find bitcoins. This is the optimal power for safe cryptocurrency mining. You can always turn off or turn on auto-collection of bonuses in your personal account in the 'settings' section.
